Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist:

- Do not click on suspicious or unknown links
- Always verify the sender’s email or message source
- Never share OTP, passwords, or personal details
- Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
- Regularly check bank statements and account activity
- Install and update antivirus software
- Ensure websites are secure (https) before entering data
- Be cautious of urgent or threatening messages

Reporting:
