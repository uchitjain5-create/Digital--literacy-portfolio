This infographic titled “Digital Literacy for Students” provides a structured overview of essential digital skills required in today’s academic environment. It explains digital literacy as the ability to effectively and responsibly use digital tools and online platforms.

The infographic highlights the importance of digital literacy in areas such as online learning, communication, career development, and maintaining online safety. It also presents key safe internet practices, including using strong passwords, avoiding suspicious links, and protecting personal information.

Additionally, the infographic introduces commonly used digital tools like Google Docs, GitHub, Canva, and LinkedIn, which support productivity and professional growth. A section on email etiquette is also included, focusing on writing clear, polite, and well-structured emails.

The design was created using Canva with a clean layout, organized sections, and relevant icons to ensure clarity and visual appeal. Overall, this infographic serves as a concise and informative guide to help students understand and apply digital literacy skills effectively.
